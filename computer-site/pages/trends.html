Tiny PC's:

Intel NUC.

Intel Compute Stick.

Very small computers as small as USB flash drives for various apps and applications like gaming and personal computing.


Wearable Tech:

Apple (Smart) Watches.

Oculus Rift virtual reality headset.

Tech clothing is also a new trend.  The ability to fit built in pockets into backpacks or vests and other types of clothing allows for holding and using technology on the go by incorporating mobile devices into design.


Campus Emergency Notification Systems:

Emergency SMS text message.

(Visiplex) Emergency alert control computer.

With emergencies at schools and campuses there is a need to be able to alert and notify many individuals at once.  The campus emergency notification systems allow notifications to alert students by mobile phones so text message alerts can quickly pass on information or some may have options for various social media or feeds.
